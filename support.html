Support

Need help with AeroSmooth Labs or btrd?

Use the right inbox and we’ll route it from there. For btrd, include your device, app version, and what happened if you are reporting a bug.

Contact

For app support, pilot testing, account questions, or feedback, email support@aerosmoothlabs.com.

For btrd bug reports

  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • Whether it was a real flight, simulator session, or ground test
  • Any screenshot or short description of the landing result

Safety note

btrd is intended for post-flight review and training discussion. It is not an FAA-approved flight instrument, navigation source, aircraft system, or replacement for instructor judgment.
