Why it exists

Better feedback without overcomplicating the flight.

Pilots already talk about greasers, firm arrivals, short-field braking, and “how did that one feel?” btrd gives that conversation a simple data-backed reference point.

  • Post-flight landing score and timeline
  • Touchdown, rollout, and braking context shown visually
  • Designed for students, renters, CFIs, and flying friends
  • Not a flight instrument or substitute for instruction
