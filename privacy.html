Privacy

Privacy policy for AeroSmooth Labs.

This page summarizes how we think about privacy for AeroSmooth Labs products, including btrd. Last updated: June 4, 2026.

AeroSmooth Labs builds aviation software, starting with btrd. We collect only what is needed to provide the product, improve reliability, and support users.

Information we may collect

  • Account or profile information you provide, such as username or email.
  • App activity needed to save landings, scores, profiles, communities, or leaderboards.
  • Device, app, and diagnostic information used to improve reliability.
  • For btrd, motion/sensor and landing-session data used to detect and review landing events.
  • Airport or flight-context information when you choose to record or save a landing.

How we use information

  • To operate app features such as landing review, scoring, profiles, and community views.
  • To troubleshoot issues, improve detection quality, and make the app more useful.
  • To respond to support, privacy, and business inquiries.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use btrd as an approved flight instrument, navigation source, or aircraft safety system.
  • We do not want sensitive personal information sent through support unless needed for troubleshooting.

Data sharing

We may use service providers for hosting, email, analytics, crash reporting, or app distribution. We use those providers to operate the product, not to sell user data.

Data choices and deletion

For account, landing, or privacy requests, email privacy@aerosmoothlabs.com. Include the email or username connected to the request so we can find the right records.

Children

Our products are not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as products evolve. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

Email privacy@aerosmoothlabs.com for privacy questions.
